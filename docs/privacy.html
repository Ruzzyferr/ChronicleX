Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ChronicleX (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use our software and related services. By using ChronicleX, you agree to this policy.

1. What we collect

ChronicleX is a content workflow tool. Depending on how you configure it, it may process:

  • Account identifiers and credentials you supply (e.g. API keys, tokens stored only on your systems).
  • Content you create or upload (e.g. text, media files) solely to perform the actions you request.
  • Technical logs on your machine (e.g. errors, paths) if you enable logging.

We do not operate a consumer social network; we do not sell your personal data.

2. How we use information

Information is used only to provide the features you enable (e.g. posting to platforms you connect, generating or editing content). Third-party platforms (such as TikTok, YouTube, or Meta) have their own privacy policies governing data they receive when you publish through them.

3. Storage and security

Credentials and content typically reside on infrastructure you control (your computer, your servers, your cloud accounts). You are responsible for securing your environment, rotating keys, and complying with applicable laws.

4. Third-party services

ChronicleX may call third-party APIs that you configure. Those services process data according to their own terms. Review each provider’s policy before connecting an account.

5. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Because deployment is often self-hosted, many requests are fulfilled by you deleting local configuration or revoking tokens at the provider.

6. Children

ChronicleX is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). Do not use it to collect data from children without lawful consent.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

For privacy questions about this application, contact the maintainer of your ChronicleX deployment (e.g. the project owner or organization that provided you the software).
